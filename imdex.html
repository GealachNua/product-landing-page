Welcome to the world of the Dance Master's Fiddle

The Dance Master from long ago was a proffesional who travelled the country teaching people how to dance. He needed music to support his lessons and since recorded dance were not available at that time he needed to provide for it himself. For that means he usally carried a small fiddle like instrument ofter mistaken as a small children fiddle, but that is not the correct. The Dance Master was a travelling man and for that he needed a small instrument suitable for easy storage and the kit violin also named the pochette was the ideal instrument. Small and not to loud. Ideal for travel en discrete dancing lessons.

The dance master's fiddle was know by several names. The name pochette was used in reference to the cloth of pochette in which it was stored. But is was also called the the kit violin.
